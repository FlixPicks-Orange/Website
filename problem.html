The Problem

Finding entertaining and engaging media is becoming increasingly tedious for people due to the rise of numerous streaming platforms and their vast libraries.

Problem Characteristics

  • There are an overwhelming number of different streaming services to choose from.
  • There is also an overwhelming amount of media on each of these platforms to choose from.
  • It can be time consuming and tedious searching for an interesting piece of entertainment through the thousands of movies, shows, and other content on these platforms.
